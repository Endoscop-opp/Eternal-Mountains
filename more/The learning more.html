Alai mauntains

On a trip to the Altai mountains, you will have an unforgettable adventure in 3 beautiful Altai mountains. The whole tour takes 15 days. The 15-day account includes the training and education of tourists, which will take no more than 3 days, accommodation and meals are included in the program at the tour base. When to go on tour, it's up to you, the time of the year, the weather, the time of the day. All necessary equipment is provided to you without timely additional payment.

Difficulty level: 4/10
