Arabian Sands

Immerse yourself in the mystical beauty of the Arabian Mountains, where ancient petroglyphs tell the stories of thousands of years, and harsh landscapes hide oases of life. This hike is a journey into the world of silent peaks, rocky valleys and spectacular sunsets that turn the rocks into fiery colors. Discover the hidden treasures of the Arabian Peninsula, where history and nature merge into one, offering a unique and unforgettable experience. Majestic mountain ranges, mysterious canyons, ancient ruins and a night sky studded with millions of stars await you. Feel the silence and peace of these places, learn about the Bedouin culture and their traditions, and find new strength in conquering the peaks of the Arabian Mountains. This hike is an opportunity to see the world from a different perspective, discover unexplored facets of beauty and feel connected to the ancient land.

Difficulty level: 1/10
